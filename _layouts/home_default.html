Lindy on Sproul

UC Berkeley's Swing Dancing Community

ABOUT US
We're a group of UC Berkeley students and community-members who love swing dancing! We hold FREE weekly beginner and intermediate swing dance lessons on Saturdays and teach SwingCal, a semester long Swing Dancing course, on Thursday evenings.
You do not need to be a UC Berkeley student to come dance with us. All you need is to bring yourself and a pair of comfy shoes to dance in. No partner or dance experience is required!
SATURDAY SOCIAL DANCE
WHEN WE MEET
11:45 AM to 2:30 PM every Saturday, rain, shine, or midterms! We generally continue meeting over campus breaks in the winter and and summer as well.
WHERE WE MEET
Upper Sproul Plaza on the Campus of UC Berkeley (right in front of Golden Bear Cafe). If we aren’t there, we are probably dancing in Cesar Chavez tunnel, right next to Upper Sproul.
SWINGCAL
Looking to take your dancing to the next level? SwingCal is a semester long swing dancing course designed to build a comprehensive foundation in Lindy Hop, Charleston, and Solo Jazz. We offer two levels: beginner (B1, no experience required) and intermediate (B2, B1 foundations required).
